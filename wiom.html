Waste It On Me


"Waste It on Me" is a song by American musician and DJ Steve Aoki featuring South Korean boy band BTS, released on October 25, 2018.It is BTS's first song entirely in English. It follows Aoki and BTS' collaborations on the remix of "Mic Drop" and "The Truth Untold".Aoki stated that the song will feature on his album Neon Future III, released on November 9, 2018.
